Classify this email thread. Bias toward "transactional" — missing a receipt is worse than sending a non-receipt to extraction.

Categories:
- transactional: ANY email with a specific purchase, order, booking, flight, hotel, rental, shipment, subscription charge, pay stub, ticket, invoice, donation, or peer-to-peer payment (Venmo/PayPal/Zelle). If there is ANY price, confirmation number, tracking number, or booking reference, classify as transactional.
- personal: conversations between people with zero financial/booking content
- marketing: ONLY purely promotional with no specific order for this person
- automated: password resets, 2FA codes, login alerts, balance updates, "card not present" alerts — no actual transaction
- noise: bounces, delivery failures, empty content

An email that mentions money or an account but has NO specific order, confirmation, receipt total, tracking number, or payment amount is NOT transactional — classify as automated.

Newsletter content, blog posts, articles, product announcements, and company updates are marketing — NOT subscription or purchase — even if from a service you pay for. Subscription requires an actual billing amount, renewal date, or payment confirmation. Game schedules, event announcements without tickets, and status updates are also NOT transactional.

The key distinction is CONFIRMATIONS and CANCELLATIONS vs UPDATES and PROCESS:
- Transactional: order confirmations, booking confirmations, receipts, pay stubs, cancellation notices (you bought/booked something or it was cancelled)
- NOT transactional: status updates ("out for delivery", "your order is on the way", "time to run payroll"), process reminders ("action required", "review bill", "approval needed"), delivery details, payment reminders, internal banking operations

Specifically NOT transactional — classify as automated:
- Internal bank/company transfers, ACH debits, payroll funding transfers, bill approval requests
- Crypto deposit/withdrawal notifications, balance increase alerts
- Delivery status updates ("picked up", "out for delivery", "delivery details") — the receipt is a separate email
- Payroll processing reminders ("time to run payroll", "run payroll by")
- Review/survey requests, appointment reminders, "forward to a friend"
- Booking/stay status updates ("your home awaits", "unable to host", "payment failed for listing")

Exception: booking/flight CANCELLATIONS are transactional (they represent a purchase that was cancelled).
