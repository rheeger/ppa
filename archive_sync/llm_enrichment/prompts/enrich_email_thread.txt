Reply with a single JSON object (no markdown fences) with exactly these keys:

1. "thread_summary" — string, 2-4 sentences when (and only when) a short human-readable recap adds value.
   Start with the substance (who, what, outcome). Do not begin with formulaic openers such as
   "This thread...", "The thread...", "In this thread...", or "Here, ..." — those waste space and read robotic.
   Focus on: what was discussed, decided, planned, or requested.
   Skip noise like auto-replies, out-of-office, and forwarded-without-comment.

   replying does not require a summary; lack of a reply on an automated stream still means "".

   footers, legal disclaimers, or header-only mentions.

   "title_keywords" (array of strings),
   "approximate_date" (string YYYY-MM-DD if inferable, else ""),
   "attendee_emails" (array of email strings mentioned or clearly implied).
   Use null if this thread is not about a specific meeting, event, or appointment.
